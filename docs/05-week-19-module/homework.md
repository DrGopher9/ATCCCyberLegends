# Week 19 — Homework

Two parts: **prep** before the scrimmage, and **follow-through** after it. The follow-through is what
turns a scrimmage into improvement.

## Before the scrimmage (~30 min)
- [ ] Re-read your role and your box's First-30 checklist ([`../03-team-roles-and-comms.md`](../03-team-roles-and-comms.md))
- [ ] Confirm your box's config backup exists and you know how to restore it
- [ ] Have your detection(s) from Phase 2 ready and your service-verify command handy
- [ ] Skim the scorecard so you know what's being measured ([`../templates/scrimmage-scorecard.md`](../templates/scrimmage-scorecard.md))

## After the scrimmage (~30 min) — follow-through
- [ ] Read the completed scorecard: which dimension were we weakest on (uptime / injects / IR / first-30
      / docs / comms)?
- [ ] Write down **your** biggest personal takeaway (something you'll do differently)
- [ ] If you own a top-3 fix from the AAR, write your plan + due date for it
- [ ] Note one thing the *team* (not an individual) should do better — a seam, not a person

## Bring to Phase 4, Week 20
- The AAR's top-3 fix list (the whole team should know it)
- Your personal takeaway
- Your owned fix (if any) with a plan

> Phase 4 opens by checking: did we close last time's top-3? Come ready to answer.
