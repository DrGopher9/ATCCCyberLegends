# Week 19 — Assessment: Mini-Scrimmage + AAR (Phase 3 Exit Gate)

The team is scored on a compressed, gentle scrimmage, then must produce an honest AAR. This is a
**baseline, not a cut** — the numbers are the starting point Phase 4 improves. Combined with the Week 17
inject set, passing this is the **Phase 3 exit gate**. Score on the
[scorecard](../templates/scrimmage-scorecard.md); debrief on the [AAR template](../templates/aar-template.md).

## Phase 3 exit gate (team level)
Met when the team has: (1) completed a timed inject set to standard (Week 17), **and** (2) run a
mini-scrimmage as a coordinated unit with an honest AAR + owned top-3 fixes (this week).

- **Gate met →** proceed to Phase 4 (Live-Fire), where intensity ramps and the capstone thresholds must
  be hit by the Week-24 dress rehearsal.
- **Not yet →** if the team couldn't function as a unit (comms collapsed, injects ignored, docs absent),
  repeat a mini-scrimmage focusing on the specific seam before advancing.

> Expect a modest baseline — a team four weeks into integration should be rough. The win is that they
> operated as one, saw their gaps honestly, and know exactly what to fix. That's precisely what Phase 4
> is for.
