# Week 25 — Homework (into the Phase 5 taper)

Budget ~45 min. Light by design — you've done the work; now lock it in and protect your energy.

## 1. Close your gap (~20 min)
- [ ] If you own a Week-24 fix: demonstrate it closed (re-create the scenario, show it succeeds)

## 2. Confirm your readiness (~15 min)
- [ ] You can run your box and back up your second box, under pressure, for a full day
- [ ] Your box's runbook/baseline, detections, and config backup are current and handoff-ready

## 3. Prep for the taper (~10 min)
- [ ] Note what keeps you sharp over a 7-hour day (energy plan)
- [ ] Read [`../04-curriculum/phase-5-taper-qualifier.md`](../04-curriculum/phase-5-taper-qualifier.md)
      so you know what the taper + qualifier logistics look like

## Bring to Phase 5
- Your closed gap
- Confirmation you're green-light on your role + backup
- Anything you think the team still needs before the qualifier (last chance to raise it)
