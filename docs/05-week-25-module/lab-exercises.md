# Week 25 — Final Gap-Closure Drills

Short and targeted. The drills are whatever the **Week-24 dress rehearsal** exposed — close them,
demonstrate closure, and confirm the previously-weak dimensions now hold. Do **not** run a full
exhausting scrimmage; the team needs to enter the taper sharp.

---

## Close each missed threshold
Pick the drills matching the Week-24 misses (menu from [Week 21](../05-week-21-module/lab-exercises.md)):

- **Missed uptime / self-owns →** harden-and-verify + fast-recovery reps on the boxes that dropped.
- **Missed injects →** inject-under-fire reps; tighten the deadline clock.
- **Missed foothold eviction/reporting →** detect → evict → re-check + IR-report reps.
- **Weak first-30 →** timed team First-30 reps until it's clean and fast.
- **Weak docs/comms →** judge-audit + simultaneous-events reps.
- **Late-game collapse →** confirm the energy/rotation plan; a short sustained block to prove the fix.

Each gap: **re-create the failing scenario, show it now succeeds, log it closed.**

## Confirmation run (short)
A ~45-minute focused scenario that exercises **only** the previously-weak dimensions (not a full
scrimmage). Confirm they hold now. If they do, the gap is closed; if not, decide with the coach whether
one more focused session is needed before the taper.

## Green-light checklist walk-through
As a team, confirm each item (from [`assessment.md`](assessment.md) / [`../02-readiness-rubric.md`](../02-readiness-rubric.md)):
- [ ] Capstone thresholds hit (Week 24, or closed this week)
- [ ] Every member Tier 1–3 for their role
- [ ] Every box two-deep (Week 23 coverage grid)
- [ ] Team sustains a full-length day without collapse

## Done?
You've closed Phase 4 when the Week-24 gaps are demonstrably closed, the confirmation run holds, and the
green-light checklist is confirmed (or the residual risk is named + owned). Individual close-out:
[`homework.md`](homework.md).
