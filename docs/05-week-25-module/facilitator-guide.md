# Week 25 — Facilitator Guide: Final Fixes + Green-Light Decision (Phase 4 Exit)

> **Phase 4, Week 25 — the phase exit.** Built from [`../templates/module-template.md`](../templates/module-template.md).

**Rubric targets:** close any capstone thresholds missed at the Week-24 dress rehearsal; make the
**green-light decision** against the full readiness bar
([`../02-readiness-rubric.md`](../02-readiness-rubric.md)).

**Council lens:** the **Coach** and **Former Tech Lead**. This is a short, focused week: close the last
specific gaps from the dress rehearsal, confirm the green-light checklist, and hand off to the Phase 5
taper. No new content, no heavy scrimmage — the team has done the work; now confirm it and protect their
energy.

**You need:** the Week-24 scorecard + AAR top-3, the green-light checklist, and the lab for targeted
final drills.

---

## Learning objectives
By end of the week:
1. Every Week-24 top-3 gap is **demonstrably closed**.
2. The **green-light checklist** is confirmed (or the specific remaining risk is named + owned).
3. The team transitions to the taper (Phase 5) with confidence and energy intact.

## Weeknight session plan (2–3 hr)
- **0:00–0:15 — Warm-up:** a crisp team First-30.
- **0:15–0:30 — Own the dress-rehearsal AAR:** the missed thresholds on the board with owners +
  verification.
- **0:30–2:00 — Targeted final drills:** close each gap (from [`lab-exercises.md`](lab-exercises.md)),
  demonstrated, not discussed.
- **2:00–2:30 — Green-light review:** walk the checklist together; assign [`homework.md`](homework.md).

## Weekend lab plan (2–3 hr) — lighter than a scrimmage
- **Warm-up (20m):** re-drill anything still shaky.
- **Gap closure (60m):** demonstrate each Week-24 top-3 fix closed under realistic (not full-length)
  conditions.
- **Confirmation run (45m):** a short, focused scenario testing *only* the previously-weak dimensions —
  confirm they now hold. **Not a full scrimmage** — the team shouldn't be exhausted going into the taper.
- **Assessment + green-light decision (30m):** run [`assessment.md`](assessment.md); make the call.
- **Phase 4 close (15m):** acknowledge the arc from brand-new team to competition-ready. Preview Phase 5
  (taper + qualifier).

## Facilitator notes & common snags
- **Confirm, don't cram.** If the dress rehearsal went well, this week is light polish. Resist the urge
  to run another exhausting scrimmage — a fried team makes more mistakes at the qualifier than an
  under-drilled one.
- **Be honest about the green light.** If a fundamental gap remains (services couldn't hold, comms
  collapsed), say so and decide: one more focused scrimmage, or a specific plan to manage the risk at
  the qualifier. Don't paper over it.
- **Protect energy.** The qualifier is ~4–6 weeks out (Phase 5 taper spans the gap). The team should
  leave Phase 4 sharp and confident, not burned out.
- **Set up the taper.** Hand the Phase 5 owner a clean picture: what's solid, what's the residual risk,
  who's on the bubble for the starting 8.

## Definition of done (Phase 4 exit)
The Week-24 gaps are closed, the green-light checklist is confirmed (or the residual risk is named +
owned), and the team transitions to the taper. The team is competition-ready — the taper keeps it that
way.
