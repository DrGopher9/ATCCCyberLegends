## Result
- **All thresholds met →** the team is **competition-ready** (pending the individual green-light checks:
  every member Tier 1–3 for their role, every box two-deep). Week 25 is light polish + taper.
- **Thresholds missed →** Week 25 closes the specific gaps; if a miss is fundamental (e.g. couldn't hold
  services under pressure, or comms collapsed all day), the coach decides whether an extra scrimmage is
  needed before the taper. Be honest — better to find it now than at the qualifier.

## Green-light checklist (the full readiness bar)
- [ ] Capstone thresholds hit in this dress rehearsal
- [ ] Every member passed Tiers 1–3 for their role
- [ ] Every box has a cross-trained backup (Week 23)
- [ ] The team sustained a full-length day without late-game collapse

> This is what the whole season built toward. A team that hits these thresholds under qualifier
> conditions has earned the green light — and is exactly the team you want walking into the Minnesota
> qualifier.
