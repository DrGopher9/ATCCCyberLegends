# Week 24 — Homework

## Before the dress rehearsal (~30 min)
- [ ] Everything ready on your box: baseline/runbook, config backup, detections, service-verify command
- [ ] You can run your box **and** back up your assigned second box
- [ ] Rest. A full 7-hour day rewards a rested team — don't cram the night before
- [ ] Plan your own energy: water, food, when you'll take a break so a backup covers you

## After the dress rehearsal (~30 min) — follow-through into Week 25
- [ ] Read the scorecard: which capstone thresholds did we hit? Which did we miss?
- [ ] For your box: uptime %, any self-own, footholds and whether you evicted + reported them
- [ ] Where did *you* fade in the back half of the day? What will keep you sharp?
- [ ] If you own a Week-25 fix, write the plan (Week 25 is short — be specific)

## Bring to Week 25 (final fixes)
- The dress-rehearsal AAR top-3
- The specific thresholds we missed (if any) and your part in closing them
- Your energy-management plan for the real 7-hour day
