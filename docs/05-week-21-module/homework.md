# Week 21 — Homework (before Week 22)

Budget ~1 hour. Fix-it weeks only work if the fixes actually close.

## 1. Close your owned fix (~30 min)
- [ ] If you own a Week-20 top-3 fix: complete it, then **demonstrate it closed** by re-creating the
      situation that exposed it
- [ ] Write down how you verified it (what you did, what proved it works now)

## 2. Re-sharpen your rusty skill (~20 min)
- [ ] Identify the one skill you fumbled under pressure in Week 20
- [ ] Do three calm reps of it (service recovery / credential sweep / persistence hunt / IR report /
      your specialty item)

## 3. Ready for heavier (~10 min)
- [ ] Week 22 is a **heavier** red team + full inject load. Note one thing you'll do to be ready
- [ ] Confirm your box's backup + detections are current

## Bring to Week 22
- Your closed fix + how you verified it
- Confirmation your rusty skill is sharp again
