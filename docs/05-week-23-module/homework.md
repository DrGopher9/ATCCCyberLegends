# Week 23 — Homework (before the Week 24 dress rehearsal)

Budget ~1 hour. Week 24 is the full 7-hour dress rehearsal scored to the capstone thresholds — this is
your last prep week.

## 1. Close your owned fix (~20 min)
- [ ] Demonstrate your Week-22 fix is closed (re-create the failing scenario, show it succeeds)

## 2. Be a backup (~25 min)
- [ ] For the box you back up: run it solo through a First-30 + a foothold + a service recovery
- [ ] Confirm you know its coupled dependencies (e.g. AD↔mail, network↔scoring/ICMP)
- [ ] If you couldn't hold it solo, name exactly what you need and get a rep with the primary

## 3. Make your own box handoff-ready (~15 min)
- [ ] Update your box's one-page baseline/runbook so your backup could run it from it
- [ ] Confirm your backup can find your detections and your config backup

## Bring to Week 24
- Your closed fix
- Confirmation you can back up your assigned box solo
- Your box's updated runbook (for your backup)
