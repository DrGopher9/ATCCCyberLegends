# Week 27 — Homework

## Before competition day
- [ ] **Rest.** Sleep well the final nights — a rested brain avoids self-owns
- [ ] Your printed kit is packed: box runbook, first-30 steps, detections, service-verify command,
      change-log/tracker, quick-reference
- [ ] You know your role, your backup box, the comms format, and the priority order cold
- [ ] Your day-of energy plan is set (water, food, break timing with backup coverage)

## Day-of mindset (read the morning of)
- [ ] Stay calm — you've run this exact day before (Week 24). Trust the training.
- [ ] Protect the scored services above all; verify after every change; log everything
- [ ] Injects are half the points — don't let them slip while heads-down on a fire
- [ ] Professional with the White Team; no contact with the red team

## After competition — follow-through
- [ ] Contribute to the team debrief: what went well, what was hard on your box
- [ ] If we advance to **regionals**: note what to sharpen for a harder red team + bigger service load
      (feeds the [regional bridge](../05-regional-bridge-module/facilitator-guide.md))
- [ ] Whatever the result: recognize what you built — a brand-new team that competed a full qualifier

## Bring to the regional bridge (if we advance)
- Your box's biggest weakness exposed at the qualifier
- One thing the team should do better against a tougher field
