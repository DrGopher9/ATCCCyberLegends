## What's different from Week 20
- **Heavier red team:** more simultaneous footholds, aggressive re-entry, lateral movement between
  segments (PA side ↔ FTD side), targeted attacks on your weakest boxes, later-game escalation.
- **Full inject load:** more injects, tighter deadlines, more multi-part.
- **Longer:** push past the hour-3 fatigue point that showed in Week 20.
- **Tests the fixes:** the Week-21 top-3 should hold — verify they do under real pressure.

## Learning objectives
By end of the week, the team can:
1. Hold up under heavier, sustained pressure — services, injects, incidents, docs, all at once.
2. Handle **lateral movement** and aggressive re-entry (evictions must stick; coupled boxes coordinate).
3. Show measurable improvement on the scorecard vs. Week 20, with Week-21's fixes holding.

## Weekend lab plan (half-to-full day) — scrimmage + AAR
- **Set-up (20m):** boards, scorecard, roles, red team briefed to heavy.
- **Scrimmage (3–4 hr):** run per [`lab-exercises.md`](lab-exercises.md) — same runbook, heavier + longer.
- **AAR (45m):** full [AAR template](../templates/aar-template.md); **top-3 fixes → Week 23.** Confirm:
  are these *new* problems or repeats?
- **Debrief (15m):** scorecard vs. Weeks 19 and 20 — the trend line.

## Facilitator notes & common snags
- **Lateral movement is the new test.** A foothold on one box becomes creds/access to another. Watch
  whether the team connects the dots across specialists (the captain + Splunk owner should see the
  pattern). Coupled boxes (AD↔mail, network↔all) matter more now.
- **Repeats are a red flag.** If Week-20 gaps re-appear, the fix-it process is theater — make Week 23
  ruthless about *demonstrated* closure.
- **Stamina + rotation.** Heavier + longer exposes fatigue. Practice rotating backups in so the team
  sustains — Week 24 is the full 7 hours.
- **Keep scoring honest.** The trend across scrimmages is the readiness signal.

## Definition of done
The team held under heavier, longer pressure, handled lateral movement + aggressive re-entry, showed a
better scorecard than Week 20 with Week-21's fixes holding, and produced an owned top-3 fix list for
Week 23.
