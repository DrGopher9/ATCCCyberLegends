# Week 17 — Homework (solo, before the weekend lab / Week 18)

Budget ~1 hour. Injects are half the score — writing well is a competition skill, not busywork.

## 1. Write a business inject response (~25 min)
Pick one and write it to White-Team standard (professional, specific, addressed to a "CEO/manager",
one page, no team-identifying info beyond what's asked):
- [ ] A memo describing your team's incident-response process, **or**
- [ ] A risk assessment naming your two biggest risks + mitigations
- [ ] Save it as a **PDF**
- [ ] Self-grade 1–5 on: format, completeness, specificity, tone, submission

## 2. Write a technical inject response (~20 min)
For a task on your box (e.g. "add a user and apply security measures"):
- [ ] Do it (or describe exactly how), verify it works, confirm no scored service broke
- [ ] Write the response from the matching template (details + security measures + verification)
- [ ] Save as PDF

## 3. Deadline discipline (~15 min)
- [ ] Write down: what's the injects owner's job, and how do they keep the team from missing deadlines?
- [ ] Write down: when a fire and an inject deadline collide, who decides, and by what priority?

## Bring to the weekend lab / Week 18
- Your two PDF responses (we'll grade a couple as a group)
- Your self-grades
- Your answer on deadline discipline
