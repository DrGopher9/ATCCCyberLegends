# Regional Bridge — Homework

For the ~4–6 weeks between state and the Midwest Regional. Budget varies by block; keep the taper light.

## Early (close gaps + scale)
- [ ] Close your qualifier weakness on your box (demonstrate it)
- [ ] If the regional adds a new box/service near your specialty, baseline it (normal state, scored
      component, break/recover, backup)
- [ ] Deepen your cross-training: can a *second* backup also run your box?

## Mid (harder reps)
- [ ] Practice against faster injects and a tougher red team; make evictions stick under more pressure
- [ ] Confirm your detections still fire against a smarter attacker; tune them

## Late (re-taper)
- [ ] **Rest** into the event; re-pack your kit for the regional venue (onsite/travel)
- [ ] Re-confirm logistics: repo/web-proxy access, connectivity, roles, backups, energy plan
- [ ] Trust the training — you've competed a full qualifier already

## Bring to the regional
- Your closed qualifier gap
- Coverage for any new box/service near your role
- A rested, ready mindset
