# Week 16 — Homework (solo, before the weekend lab / Week 17)

Budget ~1 hour. Team ops only work if each person knows their part cold.

## 1. Know your role (~15 min)
- [ ] From [`../03-team-roles-and-comms.md`](../03-team-roles-and-comms.md), write down: your role, what
      you own, whose backup you are, and your Tier-3 track
- [ ] Write the priority order the captain triages by (protect → evict → injects → harden)

## 2. Your First-30 checklist (~20 min)
- [ ] Write your box's personal First-30 steps: exact credential sweep, persistence sweep, firewall
      rules (keep ICMP + your service), forwarder check, backup — and the command that **verifies** your
      scored service
- [ ] Time yourself doing it solo on your box; write the time down

## 3. Comms format (~15 min)
- [ ] Memorize the incident call: "Incident — [system] — [what] — I'm [containing/need help]"
- [ ] Write three realistic incident calls for your box

## 4. Captain track (captain + backup captain only) (~10 min)
- [ ] Write your triage-loop script (the 4 steps you'll say out loud)
- [ ] List the injects/White-Team touchpoints you own

## Bring to the weekend lab / Week 17
- Your box's First-30 checklist + solo time
- Your three incident calls
- (Captains) your triage-loop script
